Introduction

Diametric ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our iOS application ("App").

Diametric is a health tracking application designed to help you log and analyze your blood glucose, insulin doses, meals, and weight data. We take the privacy of your health information very seriously.

Information We Collect

Health Data (via Apple HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, Diametric reads and writes the following health data through Apple HealthKit:

  • Blood Glucose Readings - Your glucose measurements from connected devices (e.g., CGM) or manual entries
  • Insulin Delivery - Insulin dose records including basal and bolus amounts
  • Carbohydrates - Carb intake from logged meals
  • Dietary Energy (Calories) - Calorie data from meals
  • Body Mass (Weight) - Weight measurements
  • Active Energy Burned - Exercise and activity calories

Important: Health data accessed through HealthKit is never sold, shared with third parties for advertising purposes, or used for any purpose other than providing you with the App's core functionality.

Data You Provide Directly

  • Meal Logs - Food items, portion sizes, nutritional information, meal times, and meal types
  • Glucose Logs - Manual glucose readings with timestamps and optional notes
  • Insulin Logs - Insulin doses with type (rapid/long-acting), units, and timestamps
  • Weight Logs - Weight entries with dates

Account Information

  • Authentication Data - Email address used for account creation and sign-in (via Supabase Auth)
  • App Preferences - Your settings and preferences within the App

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information - Device model, iOS version, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Usage Analytics - Anonymous app usage patterns to improve the user experience

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  1. Provide Core Functionality - Display your health metrics, generate insights, identify patterns and correlations in your data
  2. Sync Across Devices - Keep your data synchronized between your devices via secure cloud storage
  3. Improve the App - Analyze anonymous usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance features
  4. Provide Support - Respond to your support requests and troubleshoot issues
  5. Process Subscriptions - Manage premium subscription features through RevenueCat

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your health data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework, which provides encryption at rest when your device passcode is enabled.

Cloud Storage

To enable sync and backup, your data is securely stored in Supabase (our cloud database provider):

  • All data is transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own data
  • Supabase servers are located in secure data centers with SOC 2 compliance

Sensitive Data

  • Sensitive credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain, Apple's secure credential storage
  • We never store your Apple Health credentials or passwords

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Your data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days
  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you uninstall the App or manually delete it

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Export

You can view all your data within the App at any time. Your health data is also accessible through Apple Health.

Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  2. Confirm deletion (requires double confirmation)
  3. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers

HealthKit Permissions

You can revoke Diametric's access to HealthKit at any time:

  1. Open the Health app on your iPhone
  2. Go to Sharing > Apps > Diametric
  3. Disable the data types you no longer want to share

Children's Privacy

Diametric is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Medical Disclaimer

Diametric is a tracking and logging tool only. It is NOT a medical device and does NOT provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions. The patterns and correlations shown in the App are for informational purposes only.
